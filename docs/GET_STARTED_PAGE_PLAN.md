# Expo — /get-started Sales Page Plan

## Master Page Structure (22 Sections)

All 22 sections mapped to Expo. This is the blueprint — copy comes next.

---

### 1. HERO
**Purpose:** Hook + promise + qualifier

- Mixed typography headline with bold/thin/size contrast
- Main hook: the "brain" concept — your restaurant can finally talk to you
- "Even if..." qualifier: "Even if you've never used software to run your restaurant"
- Social proof number visible: "Join X restaurant owners" or star rating
- Warm cream background (Anthropic style) with coral accents
- Expo logo at top

---

### 2. VIDEO (VSL)
**Purpose:** Build trust through personal delivery

- Video placeholder with play button overlay
- "Watch the 3-minute demo above" text
- "🔊 Make sure your sound is ON" micro-commitment prompt
- Thumbnail: phone screen showing Expo conversation
- When ready: 3-5 min demo showing real texting with Expo

---

### 3. AUTHORITY / CREDIBILITY
**Purpose:** Borrowed trust — "if THEY say so..."

- "Powered by" logos: Square, Plaid, Claude AI (Anthropic)
- Industry stat: "Restaurant owners who track their numbers daily are X% more profitable"
- Or a quote from a restaurant industry figure
- This isn't some random app — it's built on the same AI that powers Claude

---

### 4. FIRST CTA
**Purpose:** Catch the fast movers

- "Get Started — $49/month" button
- ~~$197/month~~ → $49/month pricing display
- "Cancel anytime. No contracts." underneath
- Inline or sticky — visible without scrolling past

---

### 5. "WHAT IF..." RHETORICAL QUESTIONS
**Purpose:** Address pain, get mental "yes" responses

Three questions restaurant owners will all answer "yes" to:
- "What if you could check on your restaurant from anywhere — just by sending a text?"
- "What if you knew your food cost, labor percentage, and bank balance before your morning coffee?"
- "What if you had a business partner who watched your numbers 24/7 and told you when something was off?"

---

### 6. PERSONAL LETTER — "From: [Name]"
**Purpose:** Shift from sales page to intimate conversation

- "From: Anthony Carbonaro, Detroit, MI"
- Origin story: built this because restaurant owners deserve better tools
- Conversational tone — short paragraphs, contractions, real talk
- "I grew up around restaurants. I watched owners work 80-hour weeks and still not know if they were making money..."
- Establishes credibility without bragging

---

### 7. CHECKBOX ENGAGEMENT
**Purpose:** Micro-commitment + pain point identification

"Check all that apply:"
- ☐ You glance at the POS at end of day but don't really analyze the numbers
- ☐ You have a shoebox (or pile) of invoices you'll get to "eventually"
- ☐ You find out your food cost when the accountant tells you — 90 days too late
- ☐ You've been burned by a supplier price increase you didn't catch
- ☐ You make staffing decisions based on gut feel, not data
- ☐ You've woken up at 2am wondering if your employee made the bank deposit

"If you checked even ONE of these — Expo was built for you."

---

### 8. THE HARD WAY
**Purpose:** Show how painful the current process is

"Here's what most restaurant owners do to stay on top of their numbers:"
1. Check the POS at close (if you remember)
2. Collect paper invoices from every delivery
3. Manually enter invoices into a spreadsheet (or don't)
4. Log into your bank app to check the balance
5. Try to calculate food cost from memory
6. Call your accountant every quarter for a $500 meeting
7. Hope your employees are making deposits
8. Guess whether you can afford to hire or fire

"Sound exhausting? It is. And most owners just... don't do most of it."

---

### 9. THE SHORTCUT — Expo's 3-Part Solution
**Purpose:** Relief + excitement — "there's a better way"

"What if there was a shortcut?"

**SHORTCUT 1: CONNECT** (60 seconds)
- One tap to connect Square. One tap to connect your bank.
- Expo pulls your sales, orders, labor, deposits, and expenses automatically.

**SHORTCUT 2: TEXT** (10 seconds)
- Ask anything: "How did we do today?" "Am I making money?" "Did Lauren make the drop?"
- Text a photo of any invoice — Expo reads every line item instantly.

**SHORTCUT 3: KNOW** (24/7)
- Morning recap at 7am with yesterday's numbers.
- Alerts when labor spikes, food cost creeps, deposits are missing, or cash flow is tight.
- A partner who never sleeps, never forgets, and always has the answer.

## DESIGN NOTES

- **Max width:** 800px for text sections (never full-width paragraphs)
- **Background:** Alternate between cream (#e8e6dc) and white sections
- **CTAs:** Full-width coral buttons (#d97757), large text, shadow
- **Typography:** Serif for headlines (matching homepage), sans for body
- **Testimonials:** White cards with border, photo + name + quote
- **Checkbox section:** Interactive (clickable checkboxes)
- **Offer stack:** Table with strikethrough values
- **Mobile-first:** Everything stacks cleanly
